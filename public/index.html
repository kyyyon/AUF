Welcome to AUF


As the first Catholic university in Central Luzon, Angeles University Foundation ensures not only professional success but total development which is anchored on Christian education that is holistic, integrated and formative.

Scholarship Facility

Angeles University Foundation (AUF) is committed to making quality education accessible to deserving students through its comprehensive scholarship programs. AUF offers a range of scholarships based on academic excellence, financial need, and exceptional talents. These scholarships provide opportunities for students to pursue their dreams and reach their full potential, regardless of their financial constraints.

Courses

Angeles University Foundation (AUF) offers a diverse range of courses designed to meet the educational needs of students. With a strong commitment to academic excellence, AUF provides programs in various fields, including health sciences, engineering, business, humanities, and more.

Global Certification

Angeles University Foundation (AUF) offers a wide array of certification programs designed to enhance your knowledge and skills. These certifications are meticulously crafted to meet industry standards and global expectations, ensuring that you're well-prepared for career success.

ACADEMIC PROGRAMS


Angeles University Foundation provides a range of educational programs, spanning from elementary education to undergraduate and postgraduate levels. Regardless of your chosen field of study, HAU is dedicated to delivering education that fosters personal growth, instills values, and enhances your skills to create a meaningful impact.
