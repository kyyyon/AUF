AUF School of Law Appoints New Assistant Dean

Effective July 16, 2023, Angeles University Foundation School of Law’s Atty. Arnold “Arnie” F. De Vera, will assume the role of Assistant Dean.

Atty. De Vera joined AUF School of Law (AUF-SOL) in 2006. His advocacies in labor laws and expertise in negotiations, training, and education have shaped the landscape of the labor sector in the country. He received his degree in Bachelor of Laws from the University of the Philippines College of Law and later completed his Master of Laws as a Global Public Service Law Scholar at the New York University School of Law. He ranked 8th in the 1996 Bar Examinations.

Atty. Marianne Beltran-Angeles will be stepping down as Assistant Dean of the AUF School of Law, having served as its college secretary (2008-2016), and assistant dean since 2016. She is one of the founding faculty of the School of Law, and will continue to serve the university as Legal Counsel. Her tenure at AUF is replete with accomplishments of note. AUF SOL ranked 1st from 2012 to 2014 (5-19 candidates). In 2015, it was recognized by the Legal Education Board as one of the 15 best performing law schools in the country, obtaining 100% passing rate for first-timers. It placed 9th in overall passing rate for 2017 and 2019, and 8th in the 2018 bar examinations. In the 2020-21 bar examinations, it ranked 1st as regards exemplary passers and 2nd in passing rate (11-50 candidates). And in the recent 2022 bar examinations, it ranked 2nd in passing rate (11-50 candidates) and produced a topnotcher (25th place).

We welcome Atty. De Vera's appointment and look forward to the AUF School of Law reaching new heights under his leadership.

The AUF School of Law was established in 2006, and is headed by Retired Justice Jose C. Vitug as Dean. It is consistently among the Top 10 law schools nationwide, and is ranked 1st in Central Luzon.
